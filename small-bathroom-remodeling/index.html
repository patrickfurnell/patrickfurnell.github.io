Small Bathroom Remodeling Guide

 
Remodeling a small bathroom presents many unique challenges, most of which is the reduced space. Changing the landscape of a small bathroom can be as simple as optimizing it for space with storage containers underneath the sink and a well-placed shelf to do a whole new addition. What you decide to do will determine how much you spend and what exactly the new bathroom will look like, still small or a huge new addition to the home. What’s most important is making sure it stays within the confines of your budget and in the scope of what your purposes are for the new space. We’ve written some guides to help inspire you and educate you on small bathroom remodeling.

2. Small Bathroom Remodel Cost Guide

When you’re remodeling a relatively small bathroom, it’s still important to keep costs and prices in the forefront of your mind. One of the major influences over the costs of remodeling a small bathroom that you will have to bear is the type of layout you have or select. If you’re going with a full remodel and the new layout will require you to do a lot of renovating, then your prices will go up significantly. Renovating includes taking down walls and putting up wall components like dividers.

Utilizing the current layout or selecting a layout that is simple and works well with your existing small bathroom will help bring down costs significantly. This is because contractors won’t have to completely redo your piping, electric wiring, or anything else. Since the largest expense with a bathroom remodel is labor, this can save quite a lot of money. Using this guide, you will be able to develop a reasonably accurate estimate of how much a small bathroom remodel costs.
