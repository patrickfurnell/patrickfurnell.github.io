Bathroom Shower Remodeling Guide: Ideas, Pictures & Costs

Get a free shower remodeling estimate!

 
Remodeling your bathroom shower can completely change the face of your bathroom. If you want to make sure the whole process goes off without a hitch, then this guide will help you keep things going smoothly.

Bathroom Shower Remodeling Ideas

It can be difficult to come up with new ideas for your bathroom shower on your own. Many people are simply not very good at visualizing things like this on their own. Developing these ideas may require a little help, and that’s where these remodeling ideas come in handy. If you take these tips into account while designing your new shower, then you may end up with something you’ll fall in love with.

Use Tile for Artistic Flair

Many people mistakenly believe that tile is a boring and bland bathroom necessity. The truth is that a bathroom remodel tile shower project can go a long way toward livening up your shower if you use it creatively. There are a number of different things that you can do with tile to bring out the character of your shower.

  • Use colored tiles in patterns to create your own unique designs
  • Use brilliantly colored tiles to make your shower stand out
  • Create a tile design that extends out from your shower as a way to help your new shower design blend into the rest of your bathroom

Customize the Shower for You

Many people assume that they need to get a certain type of tub or shower stall. The fact is there is no reason for you to settle for anything other than what you really want. Take the time to think about your dream shower and the chances are you can have it. Even if your budget doesn’t allow it, there may be some creative things you can do to get you close. Also, having a limited budget can sometimes lead to creative solutions and ideas that you may not have thought of before.

Use Cool Colors

This isn’t “cool” in the sense that the colors are interesting. This is cool in the sense that the colors should be calming, light, and not too bright. Cool colors are considered to be a happy medium between very bright and very dark colors.

Bathroom Shower Remodeling Pictures

If you hit a brick wall with your designs, then you may benefit from finding some pictures of finished bathroom showers. These pictures can give you a great idea of what is possible with your shower remodel. You should keep a few things in mind when you are searching for pictures of remodeled showers. If you keep these considerations in mind, then your search will end up being faster and much more fruitful.

Stick with Showers You Can Afford
There is nothing at all to be gained from spending hours looking at showers you wish you could have. Many people get off track like this when they search for images of remodels as a way to get ideas. While this may seem like harmless fantasizing, it can actually have a very detrimental effect on how you view your shower once it’s finished.

Find Showers that are Simple
Don’t spend a lot of time looking at photos of shower remodels that would require you to do excessive restructuring of your bathroom. If you have an unlimited budget, then this really shouldn’t be a problem for you. However, if you need to be more budget conscious, then it pays to look for showers that won’t require new plumbing or restructuring of your bathroom.

Be Open to New Things
Most importantly, you should be willing to have your preconceptions be challenged. When you are open, you are more likely to find something you’ll genuinely like.
